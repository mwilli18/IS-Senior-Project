About Us

Neigh’tions Transport is locally owned and operated by a husband/wife team. The vision for
the company started with deep personal roots.
Years after arriving to the USA as a refugee from
a war-torn country, Salah had a desire to start a business using the 15+ years of
experience he had from his country in
trucking/transport. After meeting his wife
Jessica, who directs a local nonprofit that aims
to support and educate refugee families,
their passions and experiences combined to form Neigh’tions Transport Service. Neigh’tions
Transport is working toward employment
with a holistic vision with a goal to create jobs that fight oppression and bring light to the
darkness. By giving us your business, you are partnering with us in this cause.




Fully insured up to $100,000

3-vehicle capacity

Specializing in day-trips to and from Chattanooga, TN
